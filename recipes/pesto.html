Pesto

Fresh pesto basil pasta sauce.

This recipe is often known as Genovese as well hailing from the region of Genova in Italy. It is simple and made freshly with basil, toasted pine nuts, olive oil, and parmesan.

This recipe encompasses many qualities.

    Fresh, made from plucked basil
    Made with healthy fats present in olive oil
    Parmesan is a healthier cheese option and gives a nice sharpness

Ingredients:

    Basil 10oz
    Toasted Pine Nuts 1/4 cup
    Olive Oil 1/2 cup
    Pasta 150g
    Parmesan Grated 1 cup
    Salt and Pepper to taste
    Garlic Cloves 3
    Lemon 1
    Parsely 1 bunch

Optional:
    Cherry tomatoes
    Mozarella Balls mini


Steps to Make

  1. Toast the pine nuts lighty over stove top until lightly brown
  2. Grind basil in Mortal with Pestle using Coarse salt as an abrasive
  3. Slowly drizzle olive oil and add pine nuts as components break down
  4. Boil pasta in salted water till al dente
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients and grind until saucy
  6. Serve with pasta and top with Parmesan
