Pan seared Chicken Thigh

Crisp pan seared chicken thigh, a tasty low calorie option packed with protein

Ingredients

    Boneless Chicken Thigh
    Avocado Oil 2 tbsp
    Salt and Pepper to Taste

Optional:
    Hot Sauce

Steps to Make

  1. Heat Oil over cast iron skillet. Avocado Oil is preffered for the high smoke point.
  2. Generously season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pan sear Chicken Thighs until crisp and properly seared
  4. Once temperature is at least 170degree F, set aside to rest for 3min
  5. Enjoy with hot sauce or by itself
