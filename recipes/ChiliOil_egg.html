Chili Oil Fried Egg

Sunny side up egg topped with chili oil and herbs

This egg is pan fried and steamed to get a crisp bottom with a poached yolk. Served topped with chili oil and optional herbs or pickled onions.

Ingredients

    Eggs 2
    Chili oil 2 tbsp
    Avocado oil tbsp
    Pickled Onions tsp
    Salt and Pepper to taste
    Green onions diced

Steps to Make

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan or cast iron skillet
  2. Crack egg and fry for 1 minute
  3. Lightly salt and pepper
  4. Add 2 tbsp water and cover steamed for 30sec, repeat once more
  5. Serve with a drizzle of olive oil and pickled onions, herbs optional
